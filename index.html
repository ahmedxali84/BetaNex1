AgentOps Engineer

The AgentOps Engineer is responsible for building, deploying, and maintaining AI agents that operate in real-world production environments. This role ensures that AI agents run reliably, perform optimally, and scale efficiently across business applications.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Deploy, monitor, and optimize AI agents across various platforms and environments
  • Ensure system reliability, scalability, and high performance in real-time applications
  • Troubleshoot issues, implement improvements, and maintain continuous delivery pipelines
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI solutions into business workflows
  • Track metrics and analytics to continuously improve agent efficiency and decision-making

What You Will Gain:

Hands-on experience managing AI agents in production, exposure to cutting-edge AI technologies and workflows, and opportunity to impact real-world business processes through AI automation.

Apply Now

HITL Designer

The HITL Designer develops AI systems where human expertise and AI intelligence work together seamlessly. Your role focuses on improving AI decision quality, ensuring ethical and responsible practices, and designing workflows that maximize effectiveness.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design human-in-the-loop workflows that combine automation with human oversight
  • Develop feedback loops to continuously improve AI performance and accuracy
  • Ensure transparency, usability, and ethical AI practices across systems
  • Collaborate with technical, business, and product teams to align AI behavior with user goals
  • Test, validate, and refine AI outputs to improve real-world application

What You Will Gain:

Experience in designing AI systems with human collaboration, exposure to advanced AI ethics, usability, and operational challenges, and ability to shape responsible AI solutions with tangible business impact.

Apply Now

Agentic Workflow Developer

The Agentic Workflow Developer creates intelligent, goal-driven workflows where AI agents autonomously plan, execute, and adapt tasks. This role is critical for delivering scalable AI solutions that operate independently while integrating seamlessly with existing business processes.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement multi-step agentic workflows with clear task orchestration
  • Integrate AI agents with APIs, tools, and business systems for smooth operation
  • Optimize workflows for efficiency, reliability, and scalability
  • Collaborate with engineering, product, and operations teams to deliver robust AI solutions
  • Analyze workflow performance and iterate for continuous improvement

What You Will Gain:

Hands-on experience building autonomous AI systems, exposure to end-to-end AI workflow design and execution, and opportunity to create agent-driven solutions with measurable impact.
